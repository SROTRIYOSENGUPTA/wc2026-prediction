Key Storylines from the Model
🏅
Spain's Narrow Road — Portugal, Senegal, Argentina, France
Spain's path is the hardest draw in the tournament: Portugal in R16 (51.9%), Senegal in QF (51.2%), Argentina in the SF (76.3%), and France in the Final (51.3%). Three consecutive matches at essentially coin-flip odds before dismantling Argentina — then a knife-edge Final.
🇲🇽
Mexico Silences England — The R16 Shock
The model's biggest upset in the knockout stage: Mexico eliminates England in the Round of 16 at 61.5%. Mexico's host-country ELO boost and solid squad chemistry ends England's campaign early — Bellingham and co. go home at the R16 stage.
⭐
Yamal Effect: Spain Edges Portugal in R16
Lamine Yamal, Ballon d'Or #2 (score 0.965), gives Spain the decisive edge over Portugal in a knife-edge R16 clash — 51.9% Spain. The model's Ballon d'Or feature captures what ELO and xG alone cannot: the difference a generational talent makes in knockout football.
🔥
The Closest Final — Spain vs France at 51.3%
The Final is virtually a coin flip: Spain 51.3% over France. The two top Ballon d'Or teams — Yamal (0.965) vs Dembélé/Cherki/Doué — clash after travelling opposite sides of the bracket. Spain's Barcelona chemistry edges a French side with deeper attacking options.
🤖
ML Pipeline · 10 Features + Host Advantage
XGBoost multi-class classifier with isotonic calibration. Feature importance: net xG (13.0%), Ballon d'Or diff (12.6%), intl. experience (10.8%), club chemistry (10.7%), ELO (10.5%), prestige (10.3%). New: MLS/Liga MX host familiarity post-prediction boost. Log-loss 0.44.

Argentina Leads 500k Simulation — Messi's USA Advantage
After fixing a model blind spot, Argentina tops the 500k Monte Carlo simulation at 14.46% (was 11.79%). Messi and De Paul playing in MLS means Argentina knows US venues, travel patterns, and conditions better than any other contender — now captured as a post-prediction host familiarity boost.
