Key Storylines from the Model
🏅
The "Death Half" — France, Spain & Portugal Together Again
The official draw placed France, Spain, and Portugal all in the same bracket half. Only one can reach the Final. The model predicts Spain beats Portugal in R16 (63.8%), then Belgium in QF (75.3%), then defeats France in the Semi-Final (61.4%) — Yamal vs Dembélé, both Ballon d'Or top-2, on the same side of the draw.
🇳🇴
Norway's Giant Run — Stuns Brazil, Reaches QF
The model's biggest shock: Norway edges Ivory Coast in R32 (62.8%), then stuns Brazil in a coin-flip R16 at 51.2%. Haaland's Ballon d'Or score combined with Norway's underrated ELO make them dark horses — they fall to England in the QF at 21.4%, but the run itself is the story.
⭐
Yamal Effect: Spain Beats Portugal 63.8% in R16
Lamine Yamal, Ballon d'Or #2 (score 0.965), gives Spain a decisive edge over Portugal in R16 — 63.8% Spain. The model's Ballon d'Or feature captures what ELO and xG alone cannot: the difference a generational talent makes in knockout football. Stronger than the old 51.5% edge.
🔥
Spain Dismantles Argentina in the Final — 77.9%
Spain meets Argentina in the Final — opposite halves of the bracket, only one path. Spain's combination of Yamal's brilliance, Barcelona club chemistry, and top ELO makes them the clear favourite at 77.9%. Argentina edges England in a knife-edge SF (51.6%) to get there.

ML Pipeline · 10 Features + Host Advantage
